Our Story & Expertise

Established in 1997, Nichi-In Software is a diversified IT services company with more than 25 years of experience.

We have extensive experience in handling Offshore projects from Japan, USA, and other APAC countries.

Our offerings include Offshore Services, Onsite Consulting, and Product Development.

We are one of the pioneers with a strong focus on the Japan market, and over 80% of our revenue comes from repeat customers.

Our own facility in Bengaluru, the IT Capital of India, supports all of our operations with state-of-the-art infrastructure.

Our Infrastructure

Nichi-In Software‘s self-owned infrastructure in Bengaluru is designed to support software development while creating a secure, comfortable, and collaborative workspace. With state-of-the-art access control systems and reliable power backups, we ensure that everything runs smoothly.

We take pride in our professional and innovative environment, reflected in our technology and workplace vibe. The interiors blend multicultural influences including Japanese work culture, reflecting our Indo-Japanese identity.

Our workstations are ergonomically designed and spacious, offering the perfect balance of privacy and openness for informal collaboration.
