Company's pivotal business goals are Faster Speed to the Market, Domain Knowledge and Technical Expertise that accelerates the development in terms of quality and speed and at the same time, avoiding the efforts in terms of cost and time required for internal training and skill development.

We help customers realize their business goals with the help of Staff Augmentation.

Staff Augmentation is beneficial to the customer when a particular project cannot be outsourced due to many reasons such as security issues, infrastructure issues etc., and due to which the resources need to work at the site of the customer.

How does Onsite Model Work?

Nichi-In Software's Staff Augmentation services provide skilled professionals to work under your direct management to help you develop, manage and maintain your applications. Our skilled software professionals can help you manage fluctuating skill needs and the varied staffing needs to meet your aggressive project timelines.

This model involves deputation of suitable resources from Nichi-In Software to work at the customers' location or any location as required by the customer. It can be individuals or a team of resources for a particular duration. The customer shall pay an hourly/monthly rate which is agreed upon at the time of signing of the contract. In this case, apart from the hourly/monthly fee for the resources, the customer would take care of return airfare, transportation expenses in India/ Country of work, visa charges, insurance charges, daily allowance and accommodation.
