About Salesforce

Salesforce is a leading cloud-based Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platform that helps businesses manage sales, customer service, marketing, and more. It provides a unified view of customer interactions, enabling companies to improve customer engagement, automate workflows, and make data-driven decisions. Salesforce is known for its scalability, extensive integrations, and AI-powered tools like Einstein Analytics, making it a preferred choice for businesses of all sizes.
