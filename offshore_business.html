OFFSHORE BUSINESS

We Provide Optimal IT Solutions and Services to Customers Around The Globe

"Business Models Catering to the Diverse Needs of Customers Globally."

Nichi-In Software offers distinct offshore business models that are in tune with the diverse needs of customers globally, with flexible approaches to cater to them.

Offshore Business Models have various variants tailor-made for different scenarios. Each has its own advantages and can be finalized based on the requirement and mutual understanding.

Enhance Your Business Strategy

To take flexibility to the next level, we are open to further customizing these models based on mutual discussion.

Fixed Price

Nichi-In Software proposes this model when the project scope and specifications are clear and well defined. We collaborate with customers to define deliverables and timelines to estimate time and cost.

Time & Material

Ideal when project scope is unclear or may change. Customers can alter specifications at will. Billing is done hourly/monthly. Team size can be revised to optimize project cost and duration.

Dedicated Development Center

Teams with PMs, infrastructure, and tools are formed per customer requirements. Long-term productivity increases as domain knowledge is retained. Ideal for extended contracts.

SES Model

Like Dedicated Development Center, this model provides complete control to the customer over scheduling, team composition, and resource usage as per contract.

Build-Operate-Transfer (BOT) Model

Nichi-In Software builds and operates like an extension of the customer's facility and ensures a smooth transition post-agreement. Benefits include lower costs, flexibility, reduced risk, and process expertise.
